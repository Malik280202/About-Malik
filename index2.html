Fc Barcelona has won 77 trophies sofar:27 La liga (spanish football league) and 14 inside cups of Spain league and 22 International cups such as UEFA champions league and europe supercups.The last time Barcelona won trophy in 2023 team ha become national champion and won super cup of Spain.
